  • Curve C shows a rightward shift in the curve, and maximum effect is still achieved, but just barely—indicating spare receptors are now depleted.
  • Curves D and E show that the maximum response is reduced, indicating that too many receptors are irreversibly blocked, and no spare receptors are available to maintain efficacy.
